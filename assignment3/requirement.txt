Packet Sniffing Assignment
Due Friday by 11:59pm Points 100 Submitting a file upload Available Apr 30 at 12am - May 17 at 11:59pm
For this assignment you will install Wireshark and use it to examine some network traffic.

First, go to wireshark.org and download and install Wireshark.

Next, you will use Wireshark to observe traffic between your computer and the uci.edu web server. Perform the following steps to capture the packets.

Start a Wireshark packet capture.
Go to the UCI webpage by typing "uci.edu" into the address line of your web browser.
Once the page has loaded, go to Wireshark and stop the capture.
Once you have captured the packets, find the HTTPS packets between the UCI web page and your computer. Take a screenshot which shows the descriptions of the HTTPS packets in the packet-listing window.

- It may help to know the IP address of your computer and of "uci.edu". Use nslookup to figure that out.

 

Submission

Please submit an image with the descriptions of the HTTPS packets in the packet-listing window.

To get full points, make sure you complete the assignment with this information.
  - Have a brief description of your work, your machine IP address and uci.edu IP address
  - Have proof/images and marked/highlighted that you capture HTTPS packets between the UCI web page and your machine.
